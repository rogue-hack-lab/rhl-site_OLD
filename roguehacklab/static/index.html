Welcome!

Several soggy regulars are in the process of starting a hackerspace in Medford. The Rogue Hack Lab will be a community-operated, 24-hour space and infrastructure provider for technical and creative projects and events. We share a deep, totally unfounded suspicion that if we create the space, awesome people will come do awesome stuff with us there :)

The space will open near downtown Medford this spring. Bootstrap infrastructure will include clean, comfortable work areas, a great network, whiteboards, a break area and a few tools. Project ideas include 3D printing, telepresence hacks, and software. Event ideas include programming classes, co-working days, and movie nights.

The lab will be non-profit, funded mainly by member fees. We expect that membership will cost between $50 and $75 per month, and we'll have a student-slash-hardship rate around $20 per month. Membership means: 24/7 access to the space, the ability to host events, and participation in decisions about how we allocate resources.

Right now, we're looking for people who are willing to commit (just a virtual handshake, nothing more!) to membership at those prices. If you're interested, but on the fence for one reason or another -- price too high? need to see it first? etc. -- we'd also like to hear from you. All of this is to help gauge interest and SWAG how much space and budget to plan for.

Finally: if you want to take an active role in making this happen, we could absolutely use your help!
